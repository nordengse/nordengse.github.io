about us

We are a company created in 2010, dedicated to reforms, management and implementation of projects nationally and internationally.

We take charge of the execution of the corporate image, installations and security systems demanded by each client, and the decoration and interior design of the diverse projects, manufacturing and assembling in destination (USA, Canada, Australia, Russia, Japan, EU and Arab Emirates).

Our management staff began working in 2002 with various types of works and projects, among which are mentioned:

  • Bank branches
  • Play and leisure centres (bingos, games halls, bowling alleys, etc.)
  • Commercial spaces of all types (pharmacies, veterinarian clinics, boutiques, shoe stores, etc)
  • Reforms and expansions of shopping centres
  • Reconditioning of industrial buildings
  • Concessionaires
  • Offices
  • Private dwellings

From 2010 to the present, the volume of work has been focused on Europe and other countries outside the European Union (Argentina, Chile and Brazil).
